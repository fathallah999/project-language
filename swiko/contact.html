FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click "Enroll Now". You'll be guided through our registration process where you can create an account and complete your enrollment.

What if I need to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked!

Are the courses self-paced?

Yes! Most of our courses are self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own convenience. We also offer live classes and tutoring sessions for those who prefer real-time interaction.

Do you offer corporate training packages?

Absolutely! We offer customized corporate training solutions for businesses of all sizes. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements and get a tailored quote.
